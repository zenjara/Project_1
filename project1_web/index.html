About us

House description

​ Apartments Ivan are located in a small tourist place Stanici, not far from the town of Omis. House is situated above the main road, 250 meters from the sea and pebble beach. The house is accessible by vehicle. Parking for guest vehicles is located next to the house. Center Stanici with a grocery store, cafes, restaurants and small market is distanced 20 meters from the house. We recommend the visit to city of Omis distanced 4. 5 kilometers and city of Split, distanced 29 kilometers.

Accommodation

​ In the accommodation offer are three apartments. Each apartment is equipped with air-condition and wireless internet access WiFi/LAN. The use of air-condition is unlimited and free of charge. Clean apartment, fresh bed linen and towels are waiting guests on arrival. On departure guests are not obliged to clean either arrange apartment. For guests who stay longer than 7 days in the apartment fresh bed linen and towels are handed by the host every 7th day. Minimal period in which apartment can be leased is 7 days. The apartment is available from 12:00 PM on the day of arrival until 10:00 AM on the day of departure.

About the beach

​ About 250 meters below the house is beautiful pebble beach, one of the most attractive beaches in this area. Beach is made of small pebbles and it is completely safe for the kids. Beach is accessible by stairs. On the beach there is a cafe in which you can escape from the sun and cool with the refreshing drink. First sandy beach is in city of Omis, 4. 5 kilometers from the house.
